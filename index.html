Chest Disease

Apart from COPD and tuberculosis the are are multiple diseases of chest as allergic rhinitis ,pneumonia ,occupational chest diseases, fungal infection etc. We try to to understand and educate the the patient to make proper abstinence so so as to to decrease the suffering with proper medication.

He has made academic orations in state and national level Seminars .
